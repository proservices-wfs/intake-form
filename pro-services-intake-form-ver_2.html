Candidate Fit Dimensions

Fit Dimensions are important for the ideal candidate to have as a strength. These dimensions will be used during our fit interview step. Safety, Pride, Integrity and Teamwork are Pro Services' core values and are a part of every interview we do.

SAFETY — Demonstrates an appreciation for surroundings and situations to proactively ward off potential danger, risk, or injury. Takes responsibility to position themselves and those around them in a way that leads to safe outcomes and results no matter the situation. Leads by example, pays attention, controls recognized hazards, and holds others accountable for safe work practices.

PRIDE — Has a sense of ownership and commitment in their work and shares successes with others. Demonstrates a strong sense of organizational values and acts in alignment with them. Believes that their reputation and work — how others see and judge them — play a critical role in their own and the organization's success. Believes that others' successes are important and is respectful and mindful when interacting with others. The long-term interests of others and the organization are at the center of their decisions and actions. Works tirelessly to achieve important goals, showcases quality and excellence, seeks continuous improvement, pursues growth.

INTEGRITY — Understands that acting with honesty and having strong moral principles is critical to the long-term success of the company. Demonstrates an uncompromising adherence to strong moral and ethical principles and values. Aligns conduct with what they know to be excellent. "There is no wrong way to do the right thing." Follows through on commitments, communicates openly and honestly, does the right thing, takes accountability for actions.

TEAMWORK — Demonstrates that the best plans, products, and processes emerge from a collective effort. Assumes that what others have to say is valuable and, therefore, actively seeks out others' ideas and opinions; motivates people to do their best. Collaborates with others to achieve common goals/tasks, helps others to succeed, values diverse ideas.
